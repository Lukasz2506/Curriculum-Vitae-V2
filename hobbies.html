I really enjoy spending nights outdoor. That is the main reason I start camping in the forest. The silence mixed with the rustle of trees and the sounds of animals is something anusual. Sitting close to the fire in the camp where every inch aside the temperature the temperature decreasing rapidly has something magical. Sometimes the camping is the way to meet with a friends and spend time together instead of crowded bar in the citycenter, and it works really well. It is also a good opportunity to train some survival technicqes like making fire or practice to tight a knots. There is alway somettginh tasty to eat.
