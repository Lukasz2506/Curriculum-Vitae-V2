Synthos S.A.

The first job I have got, on my last semester of UoT studies was an Application Technician in Research and Development Department in one of the biggest chemical factory in Poland, Synthos. I remember the moment when I was synthetising PU for my MoS diploma and the unknow number interrupt the silence. Because I predicted that this might be a response from my future employer, I ran onto the hall and pick up the phone. That was the circumstances I got my first job, straightly connected with studies, and I was very exciting. The next 9 months of work in Synthos was the best labolatory workshop I have ever had. I constantly took part to prepare a rubber compounds, executed a crosslinking process, and made a laboratory analysis of the samples I have prepared before, like stress-strain measures, DMA phase charts, abrasion tests, viscosity measurements and many more.

Bridgestone

My next work experience was the job in one of the biggest tires producer in the world. I thought that it might be a good opportunity to develop continously. The work itself in the Bridgestone plant was very interesting excperience for me. The production scale was enormous. I was a member of R&D and one of three people extrusion subteam. I took part in new compound extrusion trials and putting the parameters into the system. It gives me a completley different perspective of work connected with elastomers - commercial scale. Unfortumatelly due to personal reasons I have changed my work plans and returned to Gdańsk.

Dr. Oetker

Finally I I found a job in the 3City. My new workplace was Dr. Oetker ambient food plant in Gdańsk, and here I work until now. I am responsible about packaging development. The projects I take part concerns quality, savings and processing optimisations regarding a whole packaging portfolio like flexibles, folding boxes, corrugated and labels. I am in contact with packaging suppliers, preparing and managing packaging specifications, organise and coordinate a packaging trials on the production. If it is needed, I also execute a laboratory measurements like friction coefficient, sealability and others. I take part in a few big projects from the areas like packaging machine purchasing, sustainability etc. My current work gives an opportunituies where I can use various techniques from project management or problem solving methods. From a few years, due to the new environmantal legislations, a big part of my work time was filled by sustainability projects (weight opitimising, redefinging packaging structures, recyclability). I think this is very interesting area. One of the main think it teach me is to have a holistic approach aiming the goals.
