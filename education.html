I graduated Technical University on Chemistry Faculty. I have MoS degree from Material Engineery with Polymer Material Speciality.
My first level diploma workshop (Engineer degree) was connected with polyurethane composites. I made a PU composite which consists a various amouint of UHMWPE fibres. The most important measurement for this researches was hysteresis phenomena. The bigger hysteresis value the bigger energy absorbtion of material. The measurement proof that fibre composite give positives results of energy absorption comparing the the pure reference polurethane samples.
The second diploma workshop (Master of Science) regards Flame retardants in the PU compound to minimize fire risk. To reduce flammability of polymer, it were used rare metals oxides combining with melaimine phosphates. Based Buttler combution trials and its results it can be conclude that some rare metal oxides gives positive effect retarding the falems of PU materials.
