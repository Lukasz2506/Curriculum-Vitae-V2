Synthos S.A.

The first job I have got, on my last semester of UoT studies was an Application Technician in Research and Development Department in one of the biggest chemical factory in Poland, Synthos. I remember the moment when I was synthetising PU for my MoS diploma and the unknow number interrupt the silence. Because I predicted that this might be a response from my future employer, I ran onto the hall a pick up the phone. That was the circumstances I got my first job, straightly connected with studies, and I was very exciting. The next 9 months of work in Synthos was the best labolatory workshop I have ever had. I constantly took part to prepare a rubber compounds, executed a crosslinking process, and made a laboratory analysis of the samples I have prepared before, like stress-strain measures, DMA phase charts, abrasion tests, viscosity measurements and many more. Beside of work I have known myself better, and I realized that from the perspective living on the second end of the country, far away from home and friends wasn't a good solution for me. That is why I started looking for a new place to develop myself...
